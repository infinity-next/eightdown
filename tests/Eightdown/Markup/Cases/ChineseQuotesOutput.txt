<p>欲速则不达<br />
(yù sù zé bù dá)<br />
More haste,less speed.</p>
<p>爱不是占有,是欣赏<br />
(ài bú shì zhàn yǒu, ér shì xīn shǎng)<br />
Love is not about possession, it's all about appreciation.</p>
<p>“您先请”是礼貌<br />
(“nín xiān qǐng” shì lǐ mào)<br />
&quot;After you&quot; is good manners.</p>
<p>萝卜青菜，各有所爱<br />
(luó bo qīng cài , gè yǒu suǒ ài)<br />
Every man has his hobbyhorse.</p>
<p>广交友，无深交<br />
(guǎng jiāo yǒu，wú shēn jiāo)<br />
A friend to everybody is a friend to nobody.</p>
<p>一见钟情<br />
(yí jiàn zhōng qíng)<br />
love at first sight. It’s generally used for people, but you can also use it for other physical objects.</p>
